Mandlebrot Set Generator

Click or tap twice to select an area to investigate.

Click or tap and hold the selected area to drag.

Hit 'Render' to zoom in on the selection.

'Back' to return the previous selection.

'Reset' returns to the original image.

The 'Iterations' slider controls the accuracy of the image. A higher number means a more accurate rendering, but slower performance.

The 'Fringe' sliders allow colored boundaries to be added. The color can be adjusted by clicking on the box under the slider.
